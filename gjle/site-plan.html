Recipes

Swiss Chicken Bake: Ingredients: 2 cups cooked, shredded chicken, 2 can cream of chicken soup, 1 cup sour cream, 1 bag frozen California blend mixed vegetables (thawed), 8 slices Swiss cheese, 2 cups French fried onions, ½ tsp Thyme, salt and pepper Instructions: Preheat oven to 350, Grease 9x13 baking dish and set aside. In large bowl combine soup, sour cream, seasonings, chicken and vegetables. Spread mixture into baking dish. Top with slices of Swiss cheese and a layer of French-fried onions. Cover with tinfoil and bake for 30 minutes or until casserole is bubbling. Remove tinfoil and bake 5 more minutes or until onions are browned. *Casserole can be built ahead (without adding the french-fried onions) and stored in fridge or freezer to cook at a later time. If frozen, thaw in fridge overnight. Top with french-fried onions prior to baking.

Tater-tot Casserole: Ingredients: 32 oz frozen tater tots, 1 pound ground beef, 2 cans cream of mushroom soup, 12 oz frozen mixed veggies, 2 cups shredded cheese, salt and pepper Instructions: Preheat oven to 350, Grease 9x13 baking dish and set aside. In large skillet brown the ground beef and drain the grease. Mix together the beef, cream of mushroom, and frozen veggies and spread into the baking dish. Top with a layer of shredded cheese and an even layer of tater tots. Bake in preheated oven for about 40 minutes or until tater tots are browned and the casserole is bubbling. *Casserole can be built ahead and stored in fridge or freezer to cook at a later time. If frozen, thaw in fridge

Crockpot Pantry Chili: Ingredients: 1 can diced tomatoes, 1 can chili beans, 1 can black beans(rinsed), 1 can condensed tomato soup, 1 can roast beef, 1 can pulled pork in BBQ sauce, 2 Tbsp dried onion, 3 Tbsp sugar, 1 Tbsp chili powder, 1 tsp ground cumin, ½ tsp ground cinnamon, ¼ tsp cayenne powder Instructions: Combine all ingredients in crockpot and cook on low for 4-6 hours
